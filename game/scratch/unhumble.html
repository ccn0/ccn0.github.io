Instructions

  • WASD or Arrow Keys to move

    E or X to craft
    Q to build
    1-9 including 0 to hold a certain item
    More In-Depth Tutorial by clicking that button.

    A game where you enjoy life, with a twist. Build anything you want,
    literally anything. Beat bosses, collect items, build beautiful
    structures. Make sure to make many arrows on the first 2 days.

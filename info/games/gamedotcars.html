    About

  • game.cars is a game where you try to avoid and eliminate a red dot. you can use bullets to stun it, and build green lines to avoid its bullets. it can also build yellow lines to avoid your bullets. there are 6 kinds of power ups, green to get 1 to 5 ammo, purple to get 5 to 20 ammo, orange turns your line green for 20 seconds and gives you 5 ammo, light blue removes 5 to your 100 of the red dots health and gives you 5 ammo, yellow stops the red dot from hiding its line and dot and removes your ammo times 2 of red dots health, dark blue teleports red dot away from you. it was based of tron and was initially a test to make a line-scanner, 'bliner', but became a game.

    • Controls

  • PC

    [W,A,S,D] to face left, right, up, or down
    [SPACE] to shoot
    [C] to build a green line.

    Mobile

    [W,A,S,D] to face left, right, up, or down
    [SPACE] to shoot
    [C] to build a green line.

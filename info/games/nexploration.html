    About

  • Nexploration is a game where you discover the land and defeat the evil covering the land in a blanket of darkness. You spawn in a 30 x 30 tile world next to a campfire. You have to keep good health, sanity, and don't die to thirst.

    • Factors

  • Health: Keep health high, if it reaches zero you die.
  • Life Force: Your maximum health, increased by beating evil.
  • Sanity: Keep high, you won't see enemies without it.
  • Thirst: Keep high, no limit, when hits zero you lose three health per second.
  • Heat: 2x time speed when high, normal when medium, losing health when zero.
  • Luck: Maximum 30, determines discovering and scavenging time, determines drops.

    • Tiles

  • Fauna Tiles: Includes all Forest, Plain, and Boulder tiles.
  • Well: Special tile where you can get water.
  • Ruined Town: Who lived here(?) Don't piss them off.
  • Werewolf Statue: Apparatus for summoning werewolf.
  • Ash Tiles: Fauna tiles, but burnt!
  • Greedy Alter: Apparatus for summoning Greedy themself.
  • Spotlight: The end!

    • Items

  • Watch: Low chance of discovering or scavenging tiles.

  • Satchel: Higher chance of discovering or scavenging tiles, especially in ruined towns and near wells.
  • Canteen: Low chance of discovering.
  • Water Dasani: Low chance of discovering, higher chance of scavenging tiles, 33% chance of obtaining water when using canteen at a well.
  • Water H2O: Low chance of scavenging, 33% chance of obtaining water when using canteen at a well, scavenging at wells.
  • Water Pepsi: Low chance of scavenging, 33% chance of obtaining water when using canteen at a well, scavenging in ruined towns and ash.
  • Crackers: High chance of discovering.
  • Milk Borden: Low chance of scavenging, high chance of discovering.
  • Milk Chocolate: Low chance of scavenging in ash.
  • Twigs: High chance of discovering.
  • Dollar Coin: Low chance of discovering.
  • Plague Coin: High chance of discovering in ruined towns.
  • First Aid: Low chance of scavenging.
  • Pills: Low chance of scavenging, high chance of scavenging in ash.
  • Silver Bullet: High chance of scavenging at wells.
  • Greedy Gem: Low chance of scavenging in ash.
  • Clover: Low chance of scavenging fauna.

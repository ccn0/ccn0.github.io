Hover

status: active | play

Hover is a game where you hover your mouse or touch and hold a circle that disappears, shape shifts, divides, brightens and darkens, and resizes for a certain amount of time to get a prize.

you could sell your self on Stocks if you qualified, you had to have 5000 or more to qualify, to get more prize points. if you did sign up, you had to give some of your money back to your investors and they would give more back. it was removed because no one played it.

there was a radio where you can listen to 5 different radio stations, qog radio station, the northern radio, laughable, and two others. laughable was notable because it featured very distorted laughing and had morse code that said "IM COMING FOR YOU QOGMAN" and had random whistling at parts.


Controls

PC
hover over the circle with mouse pointer

MOBILE
touch and hold with finger

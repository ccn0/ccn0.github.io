status: sleeping | info

Nexploration is a game where you discover the land and defeat the evil covering the land in a blanket of darkness. You spawn in a 30 x 30 world next to a campfire. You have to keep good health, sanity, and don't die to thirst.

Health: Keep health high, if it reaches zero you die. Life Force: Your maximum health, increased by beating evil. Sanity: Keep high, you won't see enemies without it. Thirst: Keep high, no limit, when hits zero you lose three health per second. Heat: 2x time speed when high, normal when medium, losing health when zero. Luck: Maximum 30, determines discovering and scavenging time, determines drops.
Fauna Tiles: Includes all Forest, Plain, and Boulder tiles. Well: Special tile where you can get water. Ruined Town: Who lived here(?) Don't piss them off. Werewolf Statue: Apparatus for summoning werewolf. Ash Tiles: Fauna tiles, but burnt! Greedy Alter: Apparatus for summoning Greedy themself. Spotlight: The end!

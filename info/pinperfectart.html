PinPerfect Art

status: active | play

PinPerfect Art is the first major game worked on by QOGGD. It was initially a test to see if lines could be added to Art Factory, but it was turned into its own game.


Controls

PC
Click-Drag to draw
L to draw a line
S to spray
Down Arrow to erase all
A Z to change thickness
W X to change hue
D C to change saturation
F V to change brightness
G B to change transperency
D+R while press flag to see dev variables
1 to hide variables
Q to open menu

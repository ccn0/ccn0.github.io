Hello Everyone,

You may have personal info or files on your Chromebook or School Device that you may want to remove before giving your device back to a school or signing out. So below i have given you some information for ChromeOS (Chromebook), or any other device (e.g. Phone, Tablet, Personal Laptop) and how to stop them from seeing those things.

  1. Chromebooks

    2. Powerwash your Chromebook. To powerwash your Chromebook to default settings, sign out. Then press CTRL + ALT + SHIFT + R. Then click where the Battery indicator is and select Restart. A box will appear that says if you want to Powerwash your Chromebook, select Powerwash, then continue. You have now reset your Chromebook to factory settings with no users or data stored on it other than the IP address.

  2. Sign Out from your device

    3. iPhone: To sign out of your phone, Go to Settings > [School Account's Name].
    Scroll down to the bottom.
    Tap Sign Out.
    Enter your Apple ID password and tap Turn Off.
    Turn on the data that you want to keep a copy of on your device.
    Tap Sign Out.
    Tap Sign Out again to confirm that you want to sign out of iCloud..
    Source

    Android: To sign out, go to Settings, then accounts, after that click/press the School Account, finally press sign out.
